Fried Rice Recipe

Quick and easy to make fried rice recipe featuring mixed vegetables, eggs and soy sauce.

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup chopped baby carrots
  • ½ cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 minced clove garlic
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups white rice
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce

Steps

  1. Place carrots in a saucepan and fill with water.
  2. Boil for 3-5 minutes while mixing in peas.
  3. Drain the water.
  4. Heat the mixture over high heat. Pour in vegetable oil, stir in carrots, peas and garlic.
  5. After 30 seconds, add eggs. Stir quickly to scramble and mix with the vegetables.
  6. Stir in cooked rice. Drizzle soy sauce while stirring to coat the rice.
