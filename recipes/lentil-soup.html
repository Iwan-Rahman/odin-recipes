Lentil Soup Recipe

Delicious lentil soup recipe packed with vegetables

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 minced cloves garlic
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 2 cups dry lentils
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • ½cup thinly sliced spinach
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat oil in large soup pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onions and carrots. Cook and stir for 3 to 5 minutes, until the onion is tender.
  3. Stir in garlic, bay leaf, oregano and basil. Cook for 2 more minutes.
  4. Stir in lentils, and add water and tomatoes. Bring to a boil and let simmer for at least 1 hour, until lentils are tender.
  5. Stir in spinach and cook until it wilts.
  6. Stir in vinegar, and season with salt and pepper.
